⚡ <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.02687">Supercharged One-step Text-to-Image Diffusion Models with Negative Prompts</a> got accepted at <strong class="aurora-text">ICCV</strong>. This work presents the **first** method to enable **negative prompting** in **one-step diffusion models**, closing a key gap between **fast generation** and **fine-grained control**. The proposed method boosts both **controllability and quality**, achieving a new **SOTA HPSv2 score**.
