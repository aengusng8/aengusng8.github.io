⚡ <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.18936">Revisit Visual Prompt Tuning: The Expressiveness of Prompt Experts</a> got accepted at <strong class="aurora-text">ICLR</strong>. This work reframes VPT through a Mixture-of-Experts lens: prompts are not just tokens, but new experts injected into attention. We show that standard VPT is limited because these prompt experts are static, and introduce VAPT, which turns them into input-adaptive experts with stronger expressiveness, fewer parameters, and optimal sample efficiency.
