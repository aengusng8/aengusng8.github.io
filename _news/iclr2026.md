✨ <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.18936">On the Expressiveness of Visual Prompt Experts</a> got accepted at <strong class="aurora-text">ICLR 2026</strong>. By formalizing the connection between **Attention and Mixture of Experts (MoE)**, we identify a key limitation in standard VPT: the **restricted expressiveness of static prompts**. To resolve this, we propose **Visual Adaptive Prompt Tuning (VAPT)**, which conditions prompt experts on the input instance. This formulation is **theoretically proven to achieve optimal sample efficiency** and yields substantial performance gains, surpassing full fine-tuning on VTAB-1K by 7.34% and outperforming VPT in low-data regimes (1% data) by over 50%, all while using fewer parameters.
