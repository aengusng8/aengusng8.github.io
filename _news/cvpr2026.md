⚡ <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24570">Anti-I2V: Safeguarding your photos from malicious image-to-video generation</a> was accepted at <strong class="aurora-text">CVPR</strong>. The paper introduces a defense against unauthorized human image-to-video generation by optimizing noise in both **CIELAB and frequency spaces** to improve robustness and target salient pixels. It also proposes **Internal Representation Collapse/Anchor** objectives to degrade temporal coherence and generation fidelity.
