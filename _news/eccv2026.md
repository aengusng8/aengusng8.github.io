⚡ <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.32020v1">Cross-Space Distillation: Teaching One-Step Students with Modern Diffusion Teachers</a> was accepted at <strong class="aurora-text">ECCV</strong>. This work removes a hidden constraint in fast diffusion distillation: **the teacher and student no longer need to live in the same latent space**. We formalize Cross-Space Distillation and introduce Bridge <span class="bridge-symbol">B<sub>&phi;</sub></span>, a lightweight latent-space interface that makes standard one-step **distillation possible across mismatched resolutions, VAEs, architectures, and diffusion/flow paradigms**.
